Empowering Youth through Innovation in Defence & Robotics

IDRO is a student-led research initiative dedicated to building indigenous defense and robotic technologies to support national security, emergency response, and societal development.

About IDRO

The Indian Defence & Robotics Organisation (IDRO) is a mission-driven platform for students and innovators focused on developing advanced technologies for drones, robotics, and autonomous systems. Inspired by national pride and driven by technological excellence, IDRO aims to make India self-reliant in defense tech through open research and collaboration.
