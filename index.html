Welcome to IDRO

IDRO is a student-led initiative driving innovation in defense technologies, robotics systems, and UAV development — inspired by the vision of a self-reliant Bharat. We aim to empower young innovators and contribute to India’s national development.

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To nurture indigenous technological capabilities among youth and deliver innovative solutions for defense and national interest.

Vision

To establish India as a global hub of defense robotics and self-reliant innovation through student-driven research and collaboration.
