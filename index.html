Welcome to IDRO

We are building India's future in defense and robotics by empowering students, uniting innovators, and developing world-class indigenous technologies. Our mission is to contribute to Bharat's self-reliance through innovation, collaboration, and youth-led research and development.

Our Vision

To build a self-reliant, secure, and technologically sovereign India by empowering students and innovators to lead the future of defense and robotics.

Inspired by the legacy of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, IDRO believes in dreaming big, building bold and defending with pride.

We envision a future where India's defense capabilities are driven by indigenous technology, student innovation and collaborative research.

We aim to become a national platform where young minds unite to develop next-gen systems in UAVs, autonomous vehicles, cyber-defense and robotics.
