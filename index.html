About IDRO

The Indian Defence & Robotics Organisation (IDRO) is a mission-driven platform for students and innovators focused on developing advanced technologies for drones, robotics, and autonomous systems. Inspired by national pride and driven by technological excellence, IDRO aims to make India self-reliant in defense tech through open research and collaboration.

We foster an environment of creativity and critical thinking, encouraging young minds to tackle real-world challenges in defence and humanitarian aid through innovative technological solutions.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

To build a self-reliant, secure, and technologically sovereign India by empowering students and innovators to lead the future of defense and robotics. Inspired by the legacy of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, IDRO believes in dreaming big, building bold, and defending with pride.

At IDRO, we envision a future where India's defense capabilities are driven by indigenous technology, student innovation, and collaborative research. We aim to become a national platform where young minds from colleges, research institutions, and the grassroots unite to develop next-generation systems in UAVs, autonomous vehicles, cyber-defense, and robotics. Our vision is to build a future where India no longer depends on imported defense tech but proudly leads with technology designed, developed, and deployed by its own people.

Our Mission

To unite and empower India's students, engineers, and researchers to design, build, and deploy indigenous defense and robotics technologies that serve the nation and secure its future.

  • Develop Indigenous Technology: Design and build UAVs, drones, robotics, and autonomous systems made in India ‚Äî for India.
  • Empower Students and Youth: Provide hands-on opportunities for students to work on real defense-tech challenges and projects.
  • Unite Innovators Nationwide: Create a collaborative platform for students, researchers, engineers, and mentors to co-develop future technologies.
  • Bridge Academia and Defense: Connect educational institutions with national defense goals through innovation, training, and research.
  • Promote Open Innovation and Learning: Encourage knowledge sharing, open-source development, and public involvement in defense R&D.
  • Honor Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam‚Äôs Legacy: Inspire youth to serve the nation through science, technology, and visionary leadership just as Kalam Sir did.
