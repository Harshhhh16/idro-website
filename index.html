Welcome to IDRO

We are building India's future in defense and robotics by empowering students and innovators to develop indigenous technologies for national security and self-reliance.

About IDRO

IDRO is a student-led national platform for indigenous defense and robotics technologies. Founded in 2025, we empower youth to build next-gen UAVs, autonomous systems, and cyber-defense tools for Bharat.

🔴 Note: IDRO is currently a non-profit and non-registered organization in its early formation phase.

Our Vision

To empower Indian youth in building self-reliant defense and robotics technologies through collaboration, innovation, and national pride.

Inspired by Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam's legacy, we aim to transform India into a global defense-tech leader.
